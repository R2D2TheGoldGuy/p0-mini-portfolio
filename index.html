About Me

My main course of study is the treehouse front-end techdegree program, I’m also taking a few self-paced courses on edX. About The Project

Welcome to my mini portfolio, I call it my 'Mini Portfolio' because its basically like codepen.io, a playground for my to tinker around with different types of technology. I create miniature projects to challenge myself to try new things and build something creative. These projects are also intended to demonstrate my skills and knowledge as part of my portfolio, not to mention that they're also projects I develop for fun. Whenever I learn new concepts and technologies, I always make sure I can apply it to a small project, this way I can get the opportunity to practice my coding skills and learn something practical while doing hands-on work. These small projects are known as mini projects, and this is where I store all of my small projects onto a small version of my portfolio website.
